Embed Settings

{# Embed color #} {# Random color #}
Whether or not you want the embed color to be random. This will override the embed color above.
{# Show author #}
Whether or not you want the embed to have an author.
{# Author name #}
The name of the author of the embed.
{# Author URL #}
The URL to link the author name to.
{# Author icon URL #}
The URL of the author's icon.
{# Embed URL #}
The URL to link the embed title to.
{# Timestamp #}
Whether or not you want the embed to have a timestamp.
{# Footer text #}
The footer text.
{# Footer icon URL #}
The URL of the footer icon.
{# Show title #}
Whether or not you want the embed to have a title.
