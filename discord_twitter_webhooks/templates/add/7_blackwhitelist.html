Whitelist

{# Whitelist active #}
Whether or not you want to use the whitelist.
{# Whitelist words #}
The words to whitelist. Only tweets containing these words will be shown if the whitelist is active. This is useful when you only want to see tweets about a specific topic.

Blacklist

{# Blacklist active #}
Whether or not you want to use the blacklist.
{# Blacklist words #}
The words to blacklist. Words in tweets containing these words will not be shown if the blacklist is active. This is useful for filtering out tweets that you don't want to see.
